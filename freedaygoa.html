Goa Itinerary - Day 1

From Ginger Hotel to Chapora, Panaji, and Big Daddy Casino

Start from Ginger Hotel

Enjoy a hearty breakfast at Ginger Hotel to energize for the day ahead.

Visit Chapora Fort

Explore the iconic Chapora Fort with stunning views of the Arabian Sea.

Return to Hotel for Lunch

Head back to the hotel for a relaxing lunch and a short rest.

Explore Panaji

Wander around Panaji's colorful streets, shop for souvenirs, and enjoy riverside views.

Return to Hotel & Get Ready

Freshen up and change into evening attire for a glamorous casino night.

Big Daddy Casino

End the night at the luxurious Big Daddy Casino for gaming and entertainment.
